About us

Fitro, a burgeoning clothing marketing company, prides itself on sourcing quality garments from two global fashion hubs: Türkiye and China. With a keen eye for trends and a commitment to affordability without compromising quality, Fitro offers a diverse range of apparel options, from chic streetwear to elegant formal attire. By importing directly from Türkiye and China, the company ensures a steady stream of fresh designs at competitive prices, captivating fashion enthusiasts worldwide."
